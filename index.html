A studio that takes
things small small.

Small Small Studio is based in Accra, Ghana. We edit objects and lighting that bridge West African craftsmanship and global design — co-created with local brass casters, wood carvers, bead makers and potters.

"small small" — a West African expression meaning to take things one step at a time, with care. It is both a philosophy and a method.

founders

Thomas Rebuffel

studio director

French-Ghanaian. Thomas leads the studio's direction, partnerships with local artisans, and the development of each series. Based full-time in Accra.

Pierre Tassin

product designer

French. Pierre drives the design process — translating material research and craft techniques into objects that hold cultural meaning and contemporary rigour.

activities

  • Design EditionsObjects produced in open and limited editions, made with Ghanaian artisans. Each piece is accompanied by documentation of the maker and technique behind it.
  • Decorative SourcingBespoke sourcing of West African craft objects and materials for architecture and hospitality projects.
  • Rural ResidencyA creative residency in Somanya, Ghana — coming soon. A space for designers, artisans and researchers to work on material and craft knowledge together.
